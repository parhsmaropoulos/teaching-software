Welcome to the help guide!

We will guide you through the app step by step with the help of images

Register-Login Step 1: Register as a student/teacher giving the required informations and use your email and password to log into the app.

Teachers:


options As a teacher you have the following options:
  • Check your profile and/or change it.
  • View the users(students) list
  • View each student grades and progress.
  • Browse through the available test and explore each one in detail.
  • Create a brand new test yourself.
Check-Profile Step 1: Click on the icon with title user-profile as show in the image.
Change-Profile Step 1: While in the profile tab, change any value you want.
Step 2: Click update button.
View user/progress Step 1: Click on the tab with title Users List.
Step 2: Browse through the list and click the button view profile.
Step 3: Scroll down, after the details of the student there are 3 sections with grades.
  • Tests (their grade, and for which number it was).
  • Final tests and their grades.
  • Progress bars.
p.s.: Progress bars show the progress of each student on their tests not their finals.
Tests Step 1: Click on the tab with title Test List.
Step 2: Iterate through the lists.The left one are the simple tests while the right list are the Final tests
Step 3: If you want to create a test click the button Create Test:
Step 4: First fill in the form with your details and the option if the test is final or not.
Step 5: If the test is not final then the main number with be fixed and cannot be changed, while if you choose to be final then you will be able to fill in whatever you want.
Step 6: If you have finished with a question click add question, if you have finished with all questions click submit test.

Students:


Options As a student u have the following options.
  • View/update your profile.
  • View Tests and take them.
  • View your progress.
  • Read all the available theories.
Veiw/update profile Step 1: Click on the icon with title user-profile as show in the image.
Step 2: Change any values you want and click the button update profile.
View Tests Step 1: Click on the icon with title test list.
Step 2: There are 2 lists of tests.The left one are the regular tests and the right one the final tests.
Step 2.5: If you have already taken a test, then there will be a message whi is saying (Completed, and will output your best grade).
Step 3:To start a test or retry it if its already completed click the button as shown in the image.
Step 4: Fill all the fiels with your answer and click End Test if you are ready.There will be a message with your grade afterwards.
View progress Step 1: Click on the icon with title user-profile as show in the image.
Step 2: Under your details there will be 3 sections with grades.
  • Normal tests grades.
  • Final tests grades.
  • Tests(normal) progress bars for each number.
Theories Step 1: To view all the available theories, click on the icon with title theories
Step 2: Click the button next to the theory you want to read.
